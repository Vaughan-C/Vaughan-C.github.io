What Makes Up Emotional Intelligence ?

  • Social skills - Working well with others
  • Empathy - Being able to see things from other peoples perspective
  • Self-regulation - Staying calm under pressure
  • Self-awareness - Knowing how your emotions affect your behaviour
  • Motivation - Keeping a positive attitude

From my research and findings, it seams EQ is mainly based on the above points. They ae listed in no particular order.

How is IQ different to EQ ?

IQ or intelligence quotient is what helps you do well in school and possibly land a decent paying job because it measures your ability to think logically and solve problems. This means you could obtain better grades in your chosen subjects thus landing a higher paying job.

EQ or emotional intelligence is what helps you handle strees, build relationships, work well with others and stay motivated. This means in a work situation with high EQ, you could possibly deal very well with other workers and make things tick along very smoothly.

Based on the differnces a simple way to descibe the two is IQ might open the door for you, but EQ is what helps you thrive and succeed after you have walked in that door.

Summary

Emotional intelligence, in my opinion is very important for us as humans because we are constantly interacting with one another in our daily lives. Having strong emotional intelligence gives us the ability to communicate better, show empathy, manage conflicts, and most importantly build healthier relationships. It is the key to creating understanding and that then creates connection. There are many tests available online that can help you work out your EQ level. I know myself after completing this research, I intent to be more conscious of this in my everyday life.
