Te Whare Tapa Whā

Te Whare Tapa Whā is a Māori model of health that depicts wellbeing as a whare (house). Its four walls are Taha Tinana (physical health), Taha Hinengaro (mental and emotional health), Taha Wairua (spiritual health), and Taha Whānau (family and social health). Together, they hold up the roof. The model is founded upon Whenua (land and roots), which provides stability and connection.
