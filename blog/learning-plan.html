Bootcamp - Learning Approach

I'll continue with the system that's proven effective for me taking my time with new concepts and being consistent with my study routine. Since I know learning takes me a bit longer, I'm not going to rush things or compare my pace to others. I'll keep doing the extra learning and practice outside of class hours because that's when things really start to click for me. My creative writing is going well, so I want to maintain that momentum and maybe even push myself a bit further in that area.

Bootcamp - Daily Structure

I'm going to stick with what's working: maintaining my training schedule (with added cardio workouts), keeping my sleep and eating habits consistent, and making sure I stay connected with my support network. These fundamentals are keeping my stress levels low and my mind clear for learning, so they're non-negotiable. I'll continue balancing my study with these wellbeing practices because I've noticed they actually help me absorb information better.

Bootcamp - Managing Challenges

When I hit a wall with understanding something new, I'll remind myself that this is normal for me and just means I need to spend a bit more time with it. I won't let it stress me out. I'll break difficult concepts down into smaller chunks and revisit them consistently until they make sense. If needed, I'll reach out for help or find additional resources to approach the material from different angles.

Bootcamp - Personal Wellbeing

While my stress levels are good overall, I'm aware that my dad's situation is on my mind. I'll make sure to check in with him regularly and start thinking practically about what support he might need. This won't interfere with my studies, but acknowledging it means it won't catch me off guard either. I'll also be mindful of how the increased cardio affects my mood and energy levels - this is my wellbeing experiment for the coming weeks.

Bootcamp - Moving Forward

My goal is simple: keep doing what I'm doing because it's working. Don't give up when things get tough, stay consistent with my routine, and trust that my system will carry me through to the end of bootcamp successfully.
